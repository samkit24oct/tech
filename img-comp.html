How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compression tool helps you reduce the file size of your images while maintaining good visual quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Select your desired output format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
  3. Adjust the quality slider to balance file size and image quality
  4. Optionally resize your image to specific dimensions
  5. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  6. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times (improves SEO ranking)
  • Reduced bandwidth usage
  • Smaller email attachments
  • Saving storage space on devices
  • Optimizing images for social media platforms

Best Image Formats

JPEG: Best for photographs and complex images with many colors.

PNG: Ideal for images with transparency or simple graphics with few colors.

WebP: Modern format offering superior compression with good quality (supported by most modern browsers).
